## W4-A — Transit station (`rail.ts`)
Upgrade the existing flat rail silhouette into a readable elevated transit line + station:
- **Supports**: regular pillars (merged) down the length holding the deck up.
- **Station platform**: a raised platform deck beside the rail at one point, with a roof
  canopy on posts.
- **Stairs / elevator block**: a stair ramp or block connecting platform to ground.
- **Train (optional)**: a simple blocky train car parked on the rail at the station
  (a few merged boxes + window band). Static is fine.
- Export the station's world position (e.g. `RAIL_STATION = {x,z}`) as a gameplay anchor.
- Keep `makeRail()` export working; keep `rail.test.ts` green + add coverage.

## W4-B — Airport (`airport.ts` NEW) centered ~(95,-105) (open NE corner)
`makeAirport(): THREE.Object3D` (Group), deterministic, merged/instanced:
- **Terminal**: a WIDE LOW building (~50w x 18d x 9h) with a glass front (curtain-wall
  band) and a flat parapet roof; a **drop-off canopy** (flat roof on posts) over the front
  curb; a blocky **"AIRPORT" sign** (simple block letters or a sign panel) on the facade.
- **Drop-off apron**: a paved slab in front (curb + a marked pickup lane).
- **Control tower**: a tall slim tower (~6x6x22) with a glass cab on top.
- **Luggage carts**: a few small instanced carts near the terminal front.
- **Car pickup curb**: a marked stand by the drop-off (export `AIRPORT_PICKUP = {x,z}`).
- Keep everything within ground bounds and clear of the E district (z>-40) and N district
  (x<35). Export `AIRPORT = {x,z}` (terminal center).
- Tests: builder returns a non-empty Object3D; exported anchors are within bounds +
  in the open NE region; determinism (same output across calls).

## W4-C — Restaurant street (`restaurantStreet.ts` NEW) centered ~(95,95) (open SE corner)
`makeRestaurantStreet(): THREE.Object3D` (Group), deterministic, merged/instanced:
- A short paved **promenade** slab with 3 **restaurant** buildings along it (reuse the
  voxel building look; warm storefronts + striped awnings + a sign band each — you may
  build small bespoke restaurant boxes here rather than going through builders.ts).
- **Outdoor seating**: instanced tables + chairs (+ umbrellas) on the promenade.
- **Menu board** + a **delivery/pickup marker** (a small stand; export
  `RESTAURANT = {x,z}` as the gameplay anchor).
- Keep within bounds, clear of E district (z>... it is at z~95 which is the S district x
  range; SE corner (95,95) is clear of both since E district is z[-30,30] and S district
  is x[-30,30]). Export `RESTAURANT = {x,z}`.
- Tests: builder returns non-empty Object3D; >=3 restaurant sub-meshes; anchor within
  bounds + in the SE region; determinism.
